Objective

Primary objective of this conference is to establish an interdisciplinary forum for the purpose of facilitating discussion and advancing the prompt dissemination of research findings and technology transfer in the fields of Manufacturing and Industrial Engineering. This platform is intended to be utilised by managers, engineers, researchers, and students alike. The conference cordially invites industries, research and development establishments, and academic institutions to participate by submitting papers and sending delegates.

Modes of participation in ICMMMI-2025

1. Registered participant:

By completing the registration form and remitting the requisite registration fees. The benefits of registering for the conference encompass the provision of technical materials, tea and coffee, as well as snacks and lunch.

2. Exhibitor

By reserving an exhibition booth for the duration of the two-day conference. As part of the conference package, two company representatives will be granted complimentary lunch coupons exclusively, in conjunction with the provision of an exhibition stall.

3. Sponsor

Through conference sponsorship, sponsors are offered complimentary registration for up to four delegates with complete conference benefits. Furthermore, the names of sponsors, along with their respective banners and logos, will be prominently featured on the backdrop and souvenir items. Sponsors will be provided the opportunity to receive substantial exposure through various forms of advertising, promotional activities, and display materials.
